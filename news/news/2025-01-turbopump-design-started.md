# Turbopump Design Started – January 2025

## Overview
Our team began development of the CAD design for our kerosene/LOX turbopump, kicking off our flagship project.

## Details
- **Design Software**: SolidWorks and ANSYS for fluid dynamics simulations.  
- **Specifications**: Centrifugal pump, targeting 1000 psi, with a titanium impeller.  
- **Next Steps**: Finalize the design and begin fabrication in Spring 2025.
